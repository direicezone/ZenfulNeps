D3 Seasonal Cheat Sheet

Leveling tips for a new season

  • Create seasonal hero, create game, take your followers weapon, leave game
  • Complete challenge rift
  • Open cache (35 DBs & other mats)
  • Level up Blacksmith & Mystic
  • Spend blood shards (Blood Shards)
  • Complete an easy boss bounty for yellow items (Zultan Kulle, Maghda, etc)
  • Go get Kanai's cube in Act 3 Ruins of Sescheron
  • Upgrade a crafted level 70 weapon in cube. Cube if usable otherwise reroll reduced level requirement by 40 (max tries 4)
  • If above step was cubable, craft a 70 2H with life on hit and a slowing effect as the secondary. Reroll (max tries 4, then craft another) the OTHER secondary to the best reduced level requirement you can get.
  • At level 8, craft new weapon and continue to upgrade to a new weapon every 5 levels
  • Buy +dmg rings and amulets at lvl 10
  • Do rifts on hard until you reach your lvl 70 weapon then move to at least T1
  • You can also do massacre bonuses in Halls of Agony, or the cleave strategy on T6
  • Craft a new 2H until you reach your lvl 70 weapon
  • Once 70, complete season journey for set and do low level GRs to get legendary gems
  • Save trash ancient items for use with Legacy of Dreams gem

What to upgrade

  • Necro 2H Scythe
  • Demon Hunter 1H Dagger
  • Witch Doctor Mojo (or VD Masks)
  • Wizard 1H Wand
  • Barb 1H Mighty Weapon
  • Monk 2H Daibo
  • Crusader Crusader Shield
